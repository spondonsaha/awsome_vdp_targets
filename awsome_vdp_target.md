# ⭐ Tips for Security Researchers

Before testing any program:

- Always read the **scope**
- Follow **responsible disclosure**
- Avoid **DoS or data destruction**
- Check **subdomains & APIs**
- Look for **authorization issues**
- Explore **GraphQL endpoints**
- Test **legacy or forgotten services**
